How it works

1. Snap QR code with your phone

Use a QR scanner app (Android, iPhone) to take a snapshot of the QR code. Follow the URL and open it in your phones browser.

2. Enter visual key

The QR code contains data to pair your phone with your dektop as well as an encryption key. These keys are encrypted with the visual verification code on the desktop and decrypted by the phone.

3. Control websites with your phone

Commands are encrypted on your phone and sent to the server. Then it get routed to your desktop where the command is decrypted and executed.
